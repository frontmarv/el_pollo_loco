El Pollo Loco

Here you can play the hero "Pepe" and fight some crazy chickens in the desert.

How to win the game:

Easy! Defeat all chickens in the level. How do you do that? I guess you have to find out on your own...

Tipp: For 10 collected coins you get 1 extra bottle of salsa

Move Left = Arrow Left / A

Move Right = Arrow Right / D

Jump = Arrow Up / W / Space

Throw Bottle = J
